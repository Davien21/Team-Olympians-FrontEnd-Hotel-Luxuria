Welcome to Luxuria

Hello there and a warm welcome to Luxuria. We’ve been expecting you! We know what it means to come from a long and tiring trip and to long for a genuine welcome, a good drink and a comfortable bed to stretch out on. If you enjoy being treated like a king, you’ve come to the right place!

Families and business people love us, too. We’ve been hosts to Kings, World Leaders, Princes, Queens etc. Our appeal to such a wide range of clientele might have to do with our attention to detail and making it all very personal. Come on in. We’re here to make you feel good!
